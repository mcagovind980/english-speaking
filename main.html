Main Point

Here are the **main points you must know for English speaking** (simple and complete):


🟢 1. Basic Sentence Structure
* Subject + Verb + Object
* Example: I eat food
* This is the foundation of speaking

🟢 2. Tenses (Very Important)
* Present: I go
* Past: I went
* Future: I will go
* Learn basic forms first, then practice daily

🟢 3. Helping Verbs
* is, am, are, was, were
* have, has, had
* will, shall
* Example: I am working, She has eaten

🟢 4. Daily Use Words (Vocabulary)
* Use simple words in daily life
* Example: eat, go, come, make, take, give
* Learn 5–10 new words daily

🟢 5. Question Making
* Use WH words: what, where, when, why, who, how
* Example: What are you doing?
* Formula: WH + helping verb + subject

🟢 6. Use of is/am/are + ing
* For current actions
* Example: I am eating, She is going

🟢 7. Practice Speaking Daily
* Speak in front of mirror
* Talk to yourself in English
* Don’t wait for perfect grammar

🟢 8. Think in English
* Stop translating from Hindi
* Make simple English thoughts

🟢 9. Common Sentences
* I am ready
* I understand
* I don’t know
* Please repeat

🟢 10. Confidence (Most Important)
* Don’t fear mistakes
* Speak slowly and clearly
* Practice regularly

🟢 11. Listening Practice
* Watch English videos
* Listen carefully and repeat

🟢 12. Real-Life Practice
* Talk with friends
* Use English in daily life

🟢 13. Avoid Common Mistakes
* Don’t say: He go → Say: He goes
* Don’t say: I am go → Say: I am going

🟢 14. Use Short Sentences First
* Start simple, then go longer
* Example: I go market → I go to the market

🟢 15. Fluency Rule
* Speak daily + listen + repeat = fluency
