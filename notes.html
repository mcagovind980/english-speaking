Notes

Here are clear, structured notes for English speaking that cover almost all the content you need to start and improve:



🗣️ 1. Basics of English Speaking


✔️ Parts of Speech


* Noun – name of a person/place/thing (e.g., boy, city)
* Pronoun – replaces noun (he, she, it)
* Verb – action (run, eat)
* Adjective – describes noun (big, happy)
* Adverb – describes verb (quickly, very)
* Preposition – shows relation (in, on, at)
* Conjunction – joins words (and, but)
* Interjection – sudden feeling (wow, oh)



⏳ 2. Tenses (Most Important)



🔹 Present Tense
* I eat food
* She plays
* I am eating
* I have eaten

🔹 Past Tense
* I ate
* She played
* I was eating
* I had eaten

🔹 Future Tense
* I will eat
* I will be eating

👉 Practice:
* I go to school
* I went to school
* I will go to school

💬 3. Daily Use Sentences



✔️ Basic Conversation
* How are you?
* I am fine.
* What are you doing?
* I am working.
* Where are you going?

✔️ Polite Sentences
* Please help me
* Thank you
* Sorry
* Excuse me



🧠 4. Common Sentence Structures



✔️ Simple Sentence
* Subject + Verb + Object
👉 I eat food

✔️ Continuous
* Subject + is/am/are + verb+ing
👉 I am eating

✔️ Perfect
* Subject + has/have + verb (3rd form)
👉 I have eaten



❓ 5. Question Making



* What are you doing?
* Where do you live?
* Why are you late?
* When will you come?

👉 Formula:
* WH word + helping verb + subject + verb

🚫 6. Common Mistakes



❌ I am go → ✔️ I am going
❌ He go → ✔️ He goes
❌ She don’t → ✔️ She doesn’t
